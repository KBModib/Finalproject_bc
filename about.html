About Sunnyside

Sunnyside agency, founded by friends who met at university, is a colourful and young studio that can make your ideas come to life. Our studio was unintentionally established in 2018 when the founders Alex Holmes, Sisanda Xaba, Kagiso Motlala, and Quinton Muzuza had to collaborate on a final project for their academic portfolios. A project they worked on earned them not only the credits they needed to graduate, but they also graduated with distinction and the client insisted they work on other projects with them. They had to register their business and decided to name it Sunnyside since it was inspired by the Sunnyside Tower building where they spent many hours working together to create what has become this amazing agency. We are based in Parkwood, Johannesburg, and feature a talented team of artists who have won international recognition.
